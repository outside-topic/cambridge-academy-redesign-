THE PROCESS

Membership Process

Join the CAP membership scheme, gain clout, and become connected with a large professional community.

Apply For Membership
APPLY ONLINE

Choose which membership is suitable for you and apply accordingly.

PAYMENT OF FEES

Once you have applied, you will receive an invoice to pay if your application is accepted.

REVIEW & VERIFY

After payment, the membership council will review your credentials. Once they are satisfied they will approve your membership.

RECEIVE CERTIFICATE

Once your membership is approved and paid, you will receive a certificate and digital badge.

Frequently Asked Questions

Any professional can become a member of Cambridge Academy of Professionals if they meet all the required criteria.

Minimum criteria for membership:
  • Associate Member: 1-3 years of experience
  • Professional Member: 3-10 years of experience
  • Fellow member: 10 years+ experience

Members can connect with the global network, attend any programme for a special fee, and much more.

Anyone can pay the membership fee through bank transfer, PayPal or credit card.

  • Associate Membership: USD 100, renewal: USD 75 per year
  • Professional Membership: USD 200, renewal USD 125 per year
  • Fellow Membership: USD 300, renewal: USD 150 per year
