Certified Function Specialist

Certified Function Specialist refers to the Professional Credential for a specific function in a profession. The Function Specialist Credential proves the competency of an incumbent to design, modify, apply and establish that particular function in an organisation.

Example:

Performance Management Specialist is a Function Specialist Credential under the Human Resources profession.

Eligibility

Criteria For CAP-Function Specialist

Cambridge Academy of Professionals has two criteria for Function Specialist Credentials; an individual must fulfil either of these to apply.

BPC

Body Of Professional Competencies

Cambridge Academy of Professionals has developed the Body of Professional Competency [BPC]. There are three professional competencies that have been developed for the CAP Function Specialist.

01

Functional
Competency

Core abilities required to perform the specific function effectively within the organization.

02

Professional
Competency

Ethical standards and professional behaviors expected in the specialized role.

03

Business
Competency

Understanding of how the function aligns with and drives overall business objectives.

Assessment Framework For CAP-Function Specialist

According to the Body of Professional Competency, there are 3 professional competencies required to become a Function Specialist. An incumbent will be evaluated through these three competency areas to become a Certified Function Specialist.
