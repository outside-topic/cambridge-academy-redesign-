Body of Professional Competency [CAP-BPC]

Cambridge Academy of Professionals developed the Body of Professional Competencies to award Professional Credentials.

5 Professional Competencies:
  • 1. Ethical Competency
  • 2. Core Functional Competency
  • 3. Business Competency
  • 4. Leadership & Strategic Competency
  • 5. Innovation & Change Competency

Eligibility Criteria For Certified Professional Credential

Cambridge Academy of Professionals has developed criteria to assess the eligibility to be nominated for a credential.

Framework

Framework For CAP-Certified Professional Credentials

The framework for CAP-Certified Professional Credentials contains 4 aspects: professional credential level, professional competency level, eligibility for each level, and assessment criteria for each level. This Framework will help with the understanding of the levels of credentials, the eligibility and assessment criteria regarding the nomination, and the preparation for how to achieve the Certified Professional Credential.

Assessment

Assessment Criteria for Certified Professional Credentials

There are five criteria to assess different levels. Each level will be evaluated through the same assessment framework.

Associate Certified Professional

Associate Certified Professional is the level-1 Credential. It is expected that the incumbent applying for this credential should be a Learner & Practitioner [LP] for each of the five criteria.

Certified Professional

Certified Professional is the level 2 credential. It is expected that the incumbent applying for this credential should be a Practitioner & Promoter [PP] for each of the five criteria.

Senior Certified Professional

Senior Certified Professional is the level 3 credential. It is expected that the incumbent applying for this credential should be a Practitioner, Promoter & Contributor [PPC] for each of the criteria.
